# Identity Lifecycle Diagram (text version)

[HIRE] --> [PROVISION ACCOUNT] --> [ASSIGN GROUPS] --> [ACTIVE USER]
                     |
                     v
             [ROLE CHANGE / DEPARTMENT MOVE]
                     |
                     v
        [UPDATE GROUP MEMBERSHIP + ATTRIBUTES]
                     |
                     v
           [TERMINATION / SEPARATION EVENT]
                     |
                     v
           [DISABLE ACCOUNT] --> [REMOVE GROUPS] --> [ARCHIVE USER]

Legend:
- HIRE: New employee onboarding (create account, add to default groups)
- ROLE CHANGE: Job title or department change (update groups)
- TERMINATION: Employment ends (move to "Leavers" group, disable)
- DATA SOURCES: users.csv, groups.csv, mapping.json
